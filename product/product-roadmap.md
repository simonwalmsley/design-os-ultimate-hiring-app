# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Authentication & Onboarding
Sign in, sign up, approval gate, business details, and how-it-works introduction.

### 2. Hiring Threads
Thread list, create and resume threads, and the phase navigation shell that holds everything.

### 3. Conversational Phases
Phases 1-3 sharing the same chat-then-review pattern: Company Profile, Intent Document, Person Profile, Job Ad, and Role Outcomes.

### 4. Rankings Dashboard
Phase 4's distinct interaction pattern: CV upload, AI scoring, leaderboard, applicant detail, and shortlist.

### 5. Admin
Admin dashboard, client management, and AI configuration.
