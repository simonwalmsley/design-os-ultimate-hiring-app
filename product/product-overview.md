# Ultimate Hiring Process

## Description
An AI-assisted web application that replaces manual hiring templates with a guided, conversational experience. It walks business owners through defining the role, writing a psychology-driven job ad, setting scoring criteria, and ranking applicants from uploaded CVs — with the human making all final hiring decisions. Built for UPA Coaching clients who are established but non-technical business owners in trades, construction, hospitality, and service industries.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Generic, undifferentiated job ads
AI generates a structured 7-section ad using green brain/red brain psychology, built from the business owner's own company profile and role requirements.

### Problem 2: Gut-feel applicant screening
AI scores every CV against weighted, role-specific criteria with written explanations, replacing subjective manual review.

### Problem 3: Inability to execute the hiring process
The app teaches UPA's methodology inline as the AI guides users step-by-step, replacing video training and Word templates that most clients can't follow.

### Problem 4: Time wasted on wrong hires
A structured process from role clarity through ranked shortlist surfaces the right candidates, reducing costly mis-hires.

## Key Features
- Phase 1: Clarity of Role (Company Profile, Intent Document, Person Profile)
- Phase 2: Job Ad Creation (7-section green brain/red brain structure with export)
- Phase 3: Role Outcomes (5 weighted scoring categories with priority ordering)
- Phase 4: Ranking (CV upload, AI-assisted scoring, dashboard with threshold and shortlist)
- AI teaches methodology inline — no external training required
- Read-only AI outputs with per-section refinement (clients direct, can't break the process)
- Model-agnostic AI layer (swap providers from admin UI)
